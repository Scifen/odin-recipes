All-Fruit Smoothies


Description

A quick and easy smoothie made entirely with fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 large banana, cut in chunks
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Directions

  1. Pour pineapple juice into a blender and add banana, strawberries, and blueberries. Cover and blend until smooth, for about 1 minute. Pour into 2 glasses
